Completed Projects

2-month Internship

As part of my 2nd year BUT training, I completed my 2-month internship from 22/01/2025 to 15/03/2025 at DEVEA, a company specializing in the wholesale of computer equipment to businesses. They also offer an ERP solution called DeveaERP, designed to improve the stock management and operations of their clients.

Project Title: DEVEAERP - INCREASE YOUR BUSINESS

DeveaERP represents an advanced business management solution, offering a comprehensive platform for companies of various sizes. The ERP simplifies business management processes with features such as:

  • Creation of orders, quotes, payments, preparation slips, delivery notes, and invoices
  • Stock and finance management
  • Human resources management: creation of employees, contracts, leave, tasks, expense reports, advance payments
  • High level of customization: document style, VAT, payment terms, payment types, incoterms, contract types, legal notices

Tasks Assigned During My Internship

  • Deployment and management of the project's development environment
  • Creation of PDFs for payment slips, credit notes, orders, slips, invoices, etc.
  • Addition of specific VAT rates for products in a selected category
  • Addition or modification of avatars for all ERP users
  • Management of file storage locally or in production (on server)
  • Debugging tests on the ERP before final deployment

Skills Acquired

  • Technical skills: ERP development, familiarization with the LARAVEL framework, database management, creation of dynamic PDFs, testing and debugging
  • Personal skills: teamwork, project management, meeting deadlines, adaptability

This internship allowed me to strengthen my IT skills and discover the world of business management through an innovative ERP solution.
